Parental Alienation Test

This test is designed to determine the presence of parental alienation. It consists of a series of questions related to the relationship between a parent and their child. The questions explore the child's behavior and feelings, as well as the behavior of the other parent, in order to determine if there is any evidence of intentional interference with the parent-child relationship. The results of this test can provide a starting point for further evaluation and intervention to address parental alienation and help restore a healthy relationship between the parent and child.

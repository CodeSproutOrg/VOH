WHO WE ARE?

Voices of Hope" is a community-based organization developed by individuals who have experienced trauma within the United States Family Law System. Our Facebook group is dedicated to empowering the voices and messages of hope from the healers, heroes, and victims who have overcome or are still oppressed and struggling through trauma within the Family Law System in the United States.

The main focus of our community is to empower awareness and resources to create global change and stop all abuse, trauma, and neglect that is occurring right now within millions of cases across America. All forms of domestic violence, such as abuse, oppression, or manipulation of their children, must be identified and stopped immediately.

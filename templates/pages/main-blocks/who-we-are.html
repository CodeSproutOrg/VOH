WHO WE ARE?

"Voices of Hope" - is a community-based organization developed by individuals who have experienced trauma within the United States Family law System. Facebook group is dedicated to empowering the voices and messages of hope from the healers, heroes and victims who have overcome or are still oppressed and struggling true trauma, within the family law system in the United States.

The main focus of community is to empower awareness and resources to create Global change and stop all abuse trauma and neglect that is occurring right now within millions of cases across America. All forms of domestic violence like abuse, oppression or manipulation of their children must be identified and stopped immediately.
